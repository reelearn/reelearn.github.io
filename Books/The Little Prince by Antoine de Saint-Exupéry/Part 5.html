The Little Prince by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

Part 5: The Return to the Stars

As the Little Prince and the aviator form a deep bond, the Little Prince shares his longing to return to his planet and his beloved rose. The aviator helps the Little Prince build a makeshift airplane, and together they embark on a journey to find his planet. However, tragedy strikes as the Little Prince is bitten by a poisonous snake. Despite his imminent death, the Little Prince remains hopeful and shares his belief that death is merely a passage to the stars.
