The Little Prince by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

Part 2: The Fox and the Rose

During his travels, the Little Prince meets a wise fox who teaches him about taming and the importance of forming connections. The fox explains that to truly see with one's heart, one must invest time and effort into building relationships. The Little Prince also discovers a beautiful rose on his planet and learns about the complexities of love and devotion. Through these encounters, he begins to understand the significance of genuine connections and the value of cherishing those we care about.
