The Little Prince by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

Part 1: The Little Prince's Journey

The Little Prince begins his journey when he leaves his tiny planet and travels through space. Along the way, he encounters various planets and meets different inhabitants. Each encounter teaches him valuable lessons about life and human nature. He learns about the importance of friendship, the dangers of vanity, and the true meaning of love. The Little Prince's journey is not just a physical one, but also a journey of self-discovery and understanding.
