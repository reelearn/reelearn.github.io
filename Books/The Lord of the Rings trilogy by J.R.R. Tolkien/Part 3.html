The Lord of the Rings trilogy by J.R.R. Tolkien

Part 3: The Return of the King

In the final installment, the different storylines converge as the battle for Middle-earth reaches its climax. Frodo and Sam finally reach Mount Doom, but the ring's power tempts Frodo, leading to a dramatic struggle. Gollum, a creature obsessed with the ring, intervenes and in the chaos, the ring is destroyed. Sauron is defeated, and the darkness begins to fade. However, the cost of victory is high, with many lives lost in the war. Aragorn is crowned as the rightful king, bringing hope and renewal to the land. The hobbits return to the Shire, only to find it corrupted by evil. They rally their fellow hobbits and restore peace to their homeland. The story ends with Frodo, Sam, and others sailing into the West, leaving Middle-earth behind.
