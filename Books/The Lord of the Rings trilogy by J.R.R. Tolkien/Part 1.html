The Lord of the Rings trilogy by J.R.R. Tolkien

Part 1: The Fellowship of the Ring

The story begins with the introduction of the hobbit Frodo Baggins, who inherits a powerful ring from his uncle Bilbo. This ring, known as the One Ring, possesses great evil and corrupts whoever possesses it. Frodo is joined by a fellowship consisting of Gandalf the wizard, Aragorn the ranger, Legolas the elf, Gimli the dwarf, and several others. Their mission is to destroy the ring by throwing it into the fiery depths of Mount Doom. Along the way, they face numerous challenges and encounters with creatures such as orcs, trolls, and the dark lord Sauron's minions. The fellowship is eventually broken, and Frodo and his loyal friend Samwise Gamgee continue their journey alone towards Mordor.
