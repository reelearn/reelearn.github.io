And Then There Were None by Agatha Christie

Part 5: The Unraveling

With each death, the remaining guests become increasingly desperate to find the killer and escape the island. They form alliances, share their suspicions, and search for clues. But just when they think they are close to uncovering the truth, another life is taken. The tension reaches its peak as they realize that the killer is always one step ahead. As they race against time, they must confront their darkest secrets and make difficult choices to survive.
