And Then There Were None by Agatha Christie

Part 1: The Invitation

And Then There Were None by Agatha Christie is a thrilling mystery novel that starts with ten strangers receiving an invitation to a secluded island. Each of them has a dark secret from their past that they would rather keep hidden. The invitation promises an opportunity for them to confront their past sins and find redemption. Intrigued by the mysterious host, they accept the invitation and arrive on the island. Little do they know that their presence on the island is not a coincidence, and they are about to face a deadly game of justice.
