And Then There Were None by Agatha Christie

Part 6: The Final Twist

In a shocking twist, the identity of the killer is revealed. The remaining guests are left stunned as they discover that the murderer is one of their own. The motive behind the killings is exposed, and the truth about each guest's past is revealed. The survivors must confront the consequences of their actions and make a final stand against the killer. As the truth comes to light, the tension reaches its climax, leading to a thrilling and unpredictable ending that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.
