And Then There Were None by Agatha Christie

Part 2: The Disappearance

As the guests settle in, they realize that their host is nowhere to be found. They are left alone with the servants, who seem strangely distant and secretive. The tension starts to build as they begin to suspect that something sinister is going on. Their fears are confirmed when a recording accuses each of them of committing a heinous crime. Shocked and terrified, they realize that they have been lured to the island to pay for their past sins. With no way to escape, they are trapped and at the mercy of an unknown killer.
