The Da Vinci Code by Dan Brown

Part 4: The Betrayal and Revelation

Langdon and Sophie's quest takes a dramatic turn when they discover that one of their trusted allies is actually a traitor. They are betrayed by Sir Leigh Teabing, a British historian and Holy Grail enthusiast, who has been manipulating them all along. Teabing reveals his true intentions: to obtain the Holy Grail and use its secret to undermine the authority of the Catholic Church. With the help of a French police cryptographer named Bezu Fache, Langdon and Sophie narrowly escape Teabing's clutches and continue their search for the truth.
