The Da Vinci Code by Dan Brown

Part 3: The Secrets of Mary Magdalene

Langdon and Sophie learn about the controversial theory that Mary Magdalene, one of Jesus' disciples, was more than just a follower. According to the Priory of Sion, Mary Magdalene was Jesus' wife and the mother of his child, and her descendants are still alive today. This revelation challenges the traditional beliefs of the Catholic Church and puts Langdon and Sophie in grave danger. They must race against time to uncover the truth before it falls into the wrong hands. Along the way, they encounter hidden codes, ancient rituals, and historical artworks that hold the key to the secret of Mary Magdalene.
