The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes by Arthur Conan Doyle

Part 4: The Speckled Band

In 'The Speckled Band,' Holmes and Watson are consulted by a young woman named Helen Stoner, who fears for her life. Helen's sister had died under mysterious circumstances, and Helen believes that she is in danger as well. Holmes investigates the case and discovers that a venomous snake, released by the sisters' stepfather, was responsible for the deaths. The stepfather had plotted to kill the sisters in order to inherit their fortune. Holmes manages to save Helen's life and brings the stepfather to justice. This story showcases Holmes' bravery and his ability to solve seemingly supernatural mysteries through logical reasoning and scientific analysis.
