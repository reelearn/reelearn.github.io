The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes by Arthur Conan Doyle

Part 6: The Return of Sherlock Holmes

Despite his apparent demise, Holmes returns in 'The Adventure of the Empty House.' Holmes reveals that he survived the fall and spent the intervening years traveling and solving cases incognito. In this story, Holmes foils an attempt on his life by Sebastian Moran, one of Moriarty's associates. Holmes' return is met with joy and relief by Watson, and the duo resumes their partnership. The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes continues with more thrilling cases and showcases Holmes' indomitable spirit and his unwavering commitment to justice.
