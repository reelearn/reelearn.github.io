Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling

Part 2: The Chamber of Secrets

In Harry's second year at Hogwarts, a mysterious diary falls into his possession. Through the diary, he communicates with Tom Riddle, a former student who reveals the existence of the Chamber of Secrets. The chamber holds a monster that petrifies students. Harry, Ron, and Hermione investigate and discover that the monster is a basilisk controlled by a memory of Riddle. They defeat the basilisk and destroy the diary, which was a Horcrux, a vessel containing a piece of Voldemort's soul.
