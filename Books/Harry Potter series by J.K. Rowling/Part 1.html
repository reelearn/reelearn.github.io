Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling

Part 1: The Boy Who Lived

Harry Potter, a young orphan living with his cruel aunt and uncle, discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. He is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he learns about the magical world and his parents' legacy. Harry befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and together they uncover a plot to steal the Sorcerer's Stone, an object that grants immortality. With courage and teamwork, they prevent the theft and defeat the dark wizard, Lord Voldemort, who had killed Harry's parents.
