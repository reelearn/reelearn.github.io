Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling

Part 5: The Order of the Phoenix

Harry's fifth year at Hogwarts is marked by the return of Voldemort and the rise of the Death Eaters. The Ministry of Magic refuses to acknowledge Voldemort's return, leading Harry and his friends to form Dumbledore's Army, a secret group to learn and practice defensive magic. Harry faces numerous obstacles, including encounters with the Ministry, Dolores Umbridge, and his own inner struggles. The year ends with a battle at the Ministry, resulting in the death of Harry's godfather, Sirius Black.
