Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling

Part 6: The Half-Blood Prince

As Harry enters his sixth year at Hogwarts, he discovers a potions textbook belonging to the mysterious Half-Blood Prince. The book helps him excel in his studies, but Harry also learns about Voldemort's past and his quest for immortality. Dumbledore shares his knowledge with Harry and together they search for Horcruxes, objects containing pieces of Voldemort's soul. Tragedy strikes when Dumbledore is killed by Severus Snape. Harry vows to continue the fight against Voldemort, setting the stage for the final battle.
