Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling

Part 4: The Goblet of Fire

Hogwarts hosts the Triwizard Tournament, a prestigious competition between three wizarding schools. Despite being underage, Harry is unexpectedly chosen as a fourth champion. Throughout the tournament, Harry faces dangerous tasks and uncovers a plot to resurrect Lord Voldemort. The Dark Lord returns to his physical form and challenges Harry to a duel. With the help of his friends, Harry escapes and exposes Voldemort's return to the wizarding world, but at a great cost.
