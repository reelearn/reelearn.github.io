Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling

Part 3: The Prisoner of Azkaban

Harry's third year at Hogwarts is filled with excitement and danger. Sirius Black, a notorious wizard and supposed supporter of Voldemort, escapes from the wizarding prison, Azkaban. As the school year progresses, Harry learns that Sirius is his godfather and not the villain he was made out to be. Alongside his friends, Harry confronts werewolves, Dementors, and a time-traveling adventure to save Sirius from the Dementor's Kiss. The truth about Harry's parents and their friends is revealed, shedding light on the past.
