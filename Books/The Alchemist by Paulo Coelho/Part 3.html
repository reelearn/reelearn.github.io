The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

Part 3: The Oasis and the Alchemist

On his way to the pyramids, Santiago arrives at an oasis where he meets an alchemist named Melchizedek. The alchemist teaches him about the concept of the Soul of the World and the importance of listening to one's heart. Santiago falls in love with a girl named Fatima but realizes he must continue his journey to fulfill his personal legend. The alchemist becomes Santiago's mentor and guides him through a series of tests and challenges.
