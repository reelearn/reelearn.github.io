The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

Part 2: The Mysterious Englishman

In Tangier, Santiago encounters a mysterious Englishman who introduces him to the concept of the Soul of the World and the principle of Personal Legends. The Englishman is on a quest to find the Alchemist, a wise man who possesses the secret of the Philosopher's Stone. Santiago learns about the importance of omens and the interconnectedness of everything in the universe. He continues his journey towards the Egyptian pyramids, determined to find his treasure.
