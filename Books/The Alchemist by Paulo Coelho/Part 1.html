The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

Part 1: The Shepherd Boy's Dream

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho begins with the story of a young shepherd boy named Santiago who dreams of a hidden treasure in the Egyptian pyramids. Intrigued by this recurring dream, Santiago decides to embark on a journey to find his personal legend. He meets an old king named Melchizedek who advises him to follow his heart and listen to the language of the world. Santiago sells his sheep and sets off on a journey to Tangier, Morocco.
