The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

Part 5: The Revelation at the Pyramids

Finally, Santiago reaches the Egyptian pyramids, only to be captured by a group of thieves who demand to know the location of his treasure. Santiago realizes that the treasure he has been searching for all along is not material wealth, but rather the wisdom and knowledge he has gained throughout his journey. He manages to escape and returns to the oasis, where he reunites with Fatima and the alchemist.
