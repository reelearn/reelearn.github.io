To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee

Part 3: Boo Radley's Mystery Unveiled

As Scout and Jem continue to be intrigued by Boo Radley, they start receiving small gifts and find hidden treasures in a tree on the Radley property. They suspect that Boo is reaching out to them, but their curiosity turns to fear when they are attacked by Bob Ewell, Mayella's abusive father. In a twist of fate, Boo Radley comes to their rescue, killing Bob and saving their lives. Scout finally meets Boo, realizing that he is not the monster they had imagined, but a kind and misunderstood person.
